Clear. mrs Braley was Deld at 3h morn of her 3#d$ Son and 5th Child. I left them Comfortable and Came to Son Pollard*s at 7, find that he had y#e$ misfortuin to break one of his ribs yesterday. I Calld at mr Bracket*s and Town*s, find mrs Town a little better. Son Ephraim*s wife here. at ditto. Birth 4th.
