Clear. mr Lambard was Calld to See mr Swanton who had a fitt at 3h morn. I went there yesterday morn, he was better and gone to Read field. Daughter Pollard left her Sister and went to Son Ephraim*s forn. mrs Capin went home. I walkt from the bridg, Calld at Crosby*s and his Father*s Stors. at mr Carter*s, Dany*s, Fletcher*s and mr Andrews & Son Jonathan*s. [I returnd from Son Lambard*s]. receivd 3/ of mrs Swanton, an old Deb#t$ of mrs Capin 1/6.
