Cloudy and Some rain. Jonathan is gone to Winslow. his wife has no#t$ been So well as Shee Seemd yesterday. the infant much woried with wind. I made use of means which gave it relief. mrs Pierce has spent the day with my Daughter. Shee Seems more Comfortable this evening. W#m$ Trask Sleeps here. at home. my Daughter and the infant have not been sowel as I Could wish.
