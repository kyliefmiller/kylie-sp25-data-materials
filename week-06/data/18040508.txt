Clear and very warm. my patient was Safely Deld of her 9#th$ Child & 3#d$ Daughter at 3h 30m yesterday morn, and is Cleverly. I tarried till mrs Coner Came to Nurs and Came home attended [by] Harnon Barton. Daughter Ballard Came and took Tea. Son Ephraim & Prentis#s$ to wk at the school house, my husband allso. at ditto. Daughter Ballard here to Tea. Birth 4#th$. recvd 1 Gln Gin [and] 1 Bushl Corn.
