Clear. Phebe Church work#t$ here. I paid her /9#d$. I was Calld at noon to go and See Lucy Shaw (who was at mr A. Bracket*s) in Labour and was Safe Deld abou#t$ 2#h$ pm of a Daughter. Shee Declared that David Edwards was the True Father of the Child. I left her Comfortable and reach#t$ home before SunSett, find Joanna Babcock here. Shee is Come to do our work. at mr Anthony*s Bracket*s to See Lucy Shaw. Birth2#nd$. out of wedlock. mrs Trask Sleeps [here]. feerecd Decr. 4th.
