Clear. I have been Piking up wood. was Calld to See the wife of Jerry Prebble who was in Labour and was Safe Deld of a Daughter, her first Born at Sun Sett. I tarried all night. Son Lambard Sent me rum, Butter & Sugar, Tea [&C] yesterday Day. at mr Prebble*s. Birth 10#th$. recvd 11/3#d$ as a reward.
