Clear. I have baked and done other work, was Calld in hast to mr Cypher*s to See Old mrs Pollard who fell from her hors and was Carried in there. Dr Winship Came and lett blood. Shee was put into a Shais, Conducted to mr Black*s. I went & tarried all night. at mr Cypher*s & Black*s. mrs Pollard fell fromher hors, hurt her much.
