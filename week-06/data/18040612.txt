Clear till near night when i#t$ sprinkled a little rain. Cyrus been to hear Coart. I have Bak#t$ & Brewd and done Some in my Gardin. Sett 4 Cabbage plants in the middle of the day. put y#e$ tops in Cold water 1 hour then Sett them. mrs Farewel took Tea with me. her Daughter Sally here foren#n$, Brot me some Butter from her marm. Lemuell Sleeps here. at home. our Cow Calvd.
