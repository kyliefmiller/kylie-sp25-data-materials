Clear and Cold. this is one year Since my husband was taken from me and Carried to Jail. A Gloomy year it has been to me. mrs Smith here at evening about a Bag which I Challing as my own. Shee said in hearing of mrs Farewel and Jonathan Ballard [Jun#r$ ] that Shee had taken out a mark of Blue yarn. mrs Farewel, Daughter Ballard and Lemuel took Super with me. at home. Tymothy Woodward Paid mrs Farewel21#D$ 15 Cents and took her receipt. I witnesedit. Shee is to Sent his note to him when Shee goes to Son Ephraim*s.
