Snowd. Son Lambard Conducted his wife and Babe here, left them with me. they tarrie all night, Slept with me. I had a very ill turn in the night but, Blessed be the Great author of all my mercies, I got Easier. Son Ephraim Came here yesterday evening very ill with a Cold. I gave him herb Tea. at home. Daughter Lambard So kindas to Com and See me. we made Sausages. Jonathan*s wife Calld into See me.
