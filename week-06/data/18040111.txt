Snow#d$. Rachel bundled up her Cloaths and was going to Sett out in the Storm for hallowell bu#t$ Daughter Ballard Came here and inSisted that Shee Should do my washing and Clean my house. Shee Said it was as Clean as Shee found it. Shee gave me the lie Several times but did the washing however, was not quite so Sulky after noon. at home. Ephraim here at evening.
