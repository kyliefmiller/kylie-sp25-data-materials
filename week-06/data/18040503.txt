Clear foren, Cloudy afternoon. I have baked flour Bread, Joanna Brown and Brewd and went to the settlement, and got her a pair Shoes at Hamlin*s. Lathrop had a Call to the Settlement, Ephraim went down. Lydia Hollowell took Tea here. Allen & Thomas & Williams Sup#t$ and Sleep here. at home. allin and Thomas Lambard Sleephere.
