Clear and pleas#t$ till near night, began to Snow at 9h evening. mr Dingley brot his Marm Stackpol here foren. we had an agreable visit. he Came and Conducted her home at evening. Cyrus, Haman and Lydia went to Son Lambard*s at evening. I have been here alone, brot wood into the N room after 9 O Clock. at home. Old Lady Stackpol made me a visit thisDay.
