Clear. I was Calld up at 1h yesterday morn. Son Jonathan*s wife in Labour. Shee was Safe Deld at 2 of her 6th Son and 9th Child. I have assisted a little about Nursing and finisht my washing. mrs Andrews, Wiman, Cleark and Pierce were here as assistances. the Patient#s$ have been Comfortable thro the Day. at home. Birth 3#d$.
