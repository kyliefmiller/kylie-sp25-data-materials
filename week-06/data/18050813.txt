Clear. my Patient was Deld of her 4th Son and 9#th$ Child at 4h morn. I left them as well as Could be Expected and Came to Son Pollard*s at 9. he and wife are not So well as we Could wish. I reacht home at 11, Old mrs Dunpha with me. I rensed and hung out my Cloaths, gathered mustard &C. at mr Caton*s [&] Son Pollard. Birth 5th.
