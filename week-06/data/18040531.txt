Clear foren, Cloudy afternoon, raind at evening. Son Ephraim with his apprentises and mr Lathrop went from here this morn to work for mr Whitwel. Ephraim has hird a Par#t$ his house. I gave him a Bed Bolster [&] pillows & 1 Blankett & pair pillow Cases. I wish him Prosperity. Cyrus was down, Says his Father has been to work with [Ephraim]. I have been boiling my Soap. at home. Son Ephraim removd his tools and Some otherthings to mr Whitwel*s.
