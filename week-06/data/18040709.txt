Clear. I work#t$ in my Gardin and pikt Some wool. Daughter Pollard Came from Son Lambard*s here and went home. Shee informd me that mrs Jones*s Soar had been opened and that Polly Pollard had go#t$ her Self into Business. Leml here, informd that Lathrop had a Soar Opened on his face by Dr Man. at Son Jonathan*s. Daughter Pollard went home.Hannah & Sally Sleep here.
