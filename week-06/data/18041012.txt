Clear. Thomas Expired at 5#h$ and 20m yesterday morn. mrs Savage and Fletcher put his Grave Cloaths on and tarried till after Breakfast. Daughter Polly, mrs Gill, Betsy [Kumbal] helped them Sew. I have been very feeble but am there. may God Sanctify this Bereevment to us all. at ditto. Thomas Expired at 5h 20m Morn.
