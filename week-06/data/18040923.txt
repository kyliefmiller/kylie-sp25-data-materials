Clear and Cool. I attended public worship. Reverend mr Cushman performd, he Discoursed from Mat#h$ 5 C 5 vers foren#n$, afternoon from Math#u$ 10th & 35#th$. I was at Son Ephraim*s at intermition, they are well. at mr Pitts*s after Service, their John is unwel. Old Lady Cox was there. I went to Son Jonathan*s at evening, Pally Seems more Composed than I have Seen her Since Shee has been Sick. at Meeting, Son Ephraim*s & [Leut] Pitts#s$.mrs Cox there. the fros#t$ kild my Vine*s thisnight.
